Placement Procedure at NIT Uttarakhand


  1. The PTP Section of the institute sends the invitations to the recruiting companies.

  2. The companies answer the invitations by filled-up the Job/ Internship Notification Form (J/INF).

  3. The institute's PTP Section collects the students' willingness and sends their willingness to the recruiting company.

  4. The placement schedule is finalized by the PTP section of the institute in consultation with the recruiting company.

  5. Recruitment process begins and recruiting company evaluate the students.

  6. List of the selected students along with the offer letters are sent to the PTP section and recuitment session ends.
